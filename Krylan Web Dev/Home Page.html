Background

Krylen Taxi Services was established in September 2020 by Ms Lavinia Pitalot as a taxi company targeted at providing reliable and safe transportation for women in Port Moresby. However, with word spreading, men too see why Krylen is and will be the most sought-for transportation company in PNG.

Although it has only been months since it’s inauguration, Krylen has grown and is quickly becoming one of POM city’s most dependable and safest transport companies. Krylen’s vision is to become a national transport brand that all industry clients can identify with; the rest of PNG can recognize that Krylen thrives on reliability, honesty and quality.

Our Drivers

All our drivers go through an intensive screening process before they can be appointed. The following criteria determines their appointment as Krylen drivers:

  • Valid Drivers Licence
  • Public Driving Permit
  • Valid ID Documentation
  • Grade 12 as minimum qualification
  • No criminal record
  • Proven previous experience with references

Fleet Control

Krylen oﬀers a 24 hour service. Communication between our control room and all of our ﬂeet is made through a mobile phone which all drivers have fixed into the vehicles.

All our vehicles are installed with Toolbox GPS tracking and ﬂeet management system – thus enabling us to respond promptly in any emergency and make sure that all our passengers are safe. This system also enables us to monitor the live position of the vehicle, the speed and its mobility status.

All our vehicles have passenger liability insurance
